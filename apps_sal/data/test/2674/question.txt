Is the MRP of my new shoes exclusive or inclusive of taxes?

-----Input:-----
- First line will contain an integer $P$

-----Output:-----
For each testcase, print either 'Inclusive' or 'Exclusive' without quotes.

-----Constraints-----
- $100 \leq P \leq 999$

-----Sample Input 1:-----
123

-----Sample Output 1:-----
Exclusive

-----Sample Input 2:-----
111

-----Sample Output 2:-----
Inclusive
